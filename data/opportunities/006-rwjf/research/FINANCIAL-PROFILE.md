## Executive Summary

The Robert Wood Johnson Foundation is one of the five largest foundations in the United States by investment assets. With total assets exceeding $13.6 billion and annual grantmaking over $540 million, RWJF has substantial capacity for major multi-year health equity initiatives. The Foundation's scale, combined with its focused health mission, makes it a transformative partner for health equity research and programs.

### Foundation Ranking

### Investment Performance

The Foundation's investment strategy aims to "earn an investment return that, over time, equals or exceeds the sum of its annual spending, as a percentage of the Foundation's assets plus the rate of inflation."

## Budget Planning Guidance

### For Evidence for Action Applications

| Budget Category | Guidance |
|-----------------|----------|
| **New Research Support (Small)** | Up to $250,000 total |
| **New Research Support (Large)** | Up to $500,000 total |
| **Minimum Request** | $50,000 (no specified floor but typical >$100K) |
| **Duration** | Up to 36 months |
| **Indirect Costs** | Include in total (caps apply by org type) |

### Budget Components Allowed

- Staff salaries and stipends
- Participant recruitment and engagement
- Data collection and analysis
- Community review sessions
- Travel and supplies
- Indirect costs (within caps)

### Budget Components NOT Allowed

- Program implementation costs (pure service delivery)
- Individual behavior-change interventions without structural components
- Clinical trials of unapproved drugs/devices
- Facility construction or renovation
- Lobbying activities
- Substitution for existing funds

## Strategic Implications

### For Grant Applications

1. **Multi-Year Budgeting** - Plan for 24-36 month project duration
2. **Full Cost Recovery** - Include all allowable costs within indirect caps
3. **Scale Appropriately** - Match request to program parameters
4. **Sustainability Planning** - RWJF values long-term impact beyond grant period

### For Partnership Development

1. **Relationship Investment** - RWJF values long-term partnerships
2. **Technical Assistance** - Utilize available support resources
3. **Learning Orientation** - RWJF emphasizes evaluation and evidence
4. **National Network** - Connect with other RWJF grantees
