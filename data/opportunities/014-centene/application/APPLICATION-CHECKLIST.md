## Key Messaging Points

### Medicaid Population Focus
- NeighborhoodHELP serves Medicaid-eligible populations
- Many residents enrolled in Centene health plans
- Addresses transportation barriers to care

### Care Coordination
- FIU Thrive enables closed-loop referrals
- Reduces fragmentation for Medicaid patients
- Supports preventive care utilization

### Cost Value Proposition
- Preventive care reduces ED utilization
- SDOH navigation improves health outcomes
- Community-based model is cost-effective

### Recommended Framing
> "NeighborhoodHELP proposes to expand mobile health services reaching [X] Medicaid-eligible residents in Miami-Dade County, addressing transportation barriers and improving preventive care access through community-based screening and navigation..."
