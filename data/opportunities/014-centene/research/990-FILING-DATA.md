## Key Takeaways

### Centene Charitable Foundation (Primary)

1. **Substantial Assets**: $221M in assets enables significant grantmaking
2. **High Efficiency**: 98.9% of expenses go to charitable purposes
3. **Active Grantmaker**: 125 grants totaling $29.8M in 2024
4. **Declining Volume, Stable Value**: Fewer grants but similar total giving (strategic focus)
5. **Zero Officer Compensation**: Foundation overhead minimal
6. **Investment-Funded**: Operates from investment income, not ongoing corporate contributions

### Centene Foundation For Quality Health Care (Secondary)

1. **Limited Activity**: Small scale compared to Charitable Foundation
2. **Corporate Funded**: Receives contributions rather than investment income
3. **Uncertain Status**: Not on current IRS list, filing gaps exist
4. **Likely Dormant**: May be transitioning all activity to Charitable Foundation

### Recommendation

**Focus grant-seeking efforts on Centene Charitable Foundation (EIN 20-1298192)** as the active grantmaking entity. The Foundation For Quality Health Care appears to have minimal or no current activity.
