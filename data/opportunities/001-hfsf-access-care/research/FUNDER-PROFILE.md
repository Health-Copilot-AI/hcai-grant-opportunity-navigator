## Application Process

### Co-Development Model
HFSF uses a relationship-based, co-development approach:
1. Initial contact with program staff
2. Concept discussion and alignment
3. Invitation to submit (if aligned)
4. Collaborative proposal development
5. Final application and review

### Critical Requirements
- **MUST contact staff BEFORE submitting**
- Concept must align with current priorities
- Geographic focus on South Florida
- Community health improvement focus

---

## Decision Making
