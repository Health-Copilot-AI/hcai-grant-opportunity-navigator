## Concept Development Suggestions

### Alignment with Health and Wellness Priority

The Miami Foundation's "Access to Health and Wellness" priority emphasizes:
- Connecting residents to quality health resources
- Health education
- Addressing health disparities
- Social determinants of health

### Potential Project Concepts

1. **Community Health Navigation**: Expand SDOH screening and navigation services in underserved Miami-Dade communities

2. **Health Education Initiative**: Community health literacy program through NHELP mobile platform

3. **Health Disparities Reduction**: Targeted intervention addressing specific health disparities in Miami-Dade

4. **Integrated Health Services**: Connect mobile health to social services addressing SDOH

### Differentiation Strategy

- Established 15+ year presence in Miami-Dade
- Unique mobile health delivery model
- Technology-enhanced (Thrive) approach to SDOH
- FIU institutional support and sustainability
- Strong community partnerships and trust
