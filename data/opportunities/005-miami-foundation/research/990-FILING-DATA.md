## Key Insights for Grant Seekers

### Financial Strength Indicators
1. **Asset Base:** $565M+ provides stable grantmaking capacity
2. **Revenue Diversity:** Multiple revenue streams (contributions, investments, program services)
3. **Grant Ratio:** High payout relative to assets
4. **Growth Trajectory:** Consistent asset and giving growth

### Operational Considerations
1. **Trust-Based Philanthropy:** Unrestricted general operating support model
2. **Community Voice:** Community Advisors in selection process
3. **Bi-Annual Cycle:** 2-year grant periods with 2-year moratorium between awards
4. **Capacity Building:** Additional support beyond financial grants

### Risk Factors
1. **Market Dependency:** Investment income fluctuates with market
2. **High Competition:** 900+ applications for ~127 awards
3. **Moratorium Policy:** Organizations cannot apply in consecutive cycles
