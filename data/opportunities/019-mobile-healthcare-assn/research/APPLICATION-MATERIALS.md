## Overview

The Mobile Healthcare Association offers two primary engagement pathways:

1. **Organizational Membership** - Annual membership providing access to resources, networking, and grant eligibility
2. **Strengthening & Sustaining Grant Program** - Annual competitive grant program for members

**Important:** Membership is a prerequisite for grant eligibility. Organizations must be members in good standing before applying for grants.

---

## Part 1: Membership Application

### Eligibility Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Organization Type** | Nonprofits, hospitals, universities, FQHCs, health departments, foundations, community clinics, faith-based organizations |
| **Program Status** | Currently operating OR considering mobile healthcare services |
| **Geographic Scope** | Primarily U.S., also accepts Canadian organizations |

### Membership Process

1. Visit https://mobilehca.org/membership/organization/
2. Complete online membership application
3. Submit payment ($345 annual fee)
4. Receive confirmation and member portal access

### Payment Options

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Online** | Credit card via website |
| **Check** | Mail to St. Louis address |
| **Phone** | Call MHA office |

### What's Included

- Full organizational access (all staff)
- Member directory listing
- Webinar access and recordings
- Newsletter subscription
- Regional coalition participation
- Special interest group access
- Learning Lab access
- Conference registration discounts
- Grant program eligibility

## Part 2: Strengthening & Sustaining Grant Program

### Program Overview

The Strengthening & Sustaining Mobile Healthcare Grant Program, funded by The Leon Lowenstein Foundation, provides funding to help mobile health programs enhance their operations through evaluation, monitoring, and program improvement initiatives.

### Grant Details

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Award Amount** | Up to $10,000 per organization |
| **Number of Awards** | Approximately 10 annually |
| **Funder** | The Leon Lowenstein Foundation |
| **Geographic Scope** | U.S. and Canada |

### Eligibility Checklist

- [ ] Mobile Healthcare Association member in good standing
- [ ] Currently operating a mobile health program
- [ ] Program in operation for at least one year (as of application deadline)
- [ ] 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization OR county/state agency

## Application Preparation Guidance

### Focus Areas to Address

The program emphasizes building evaluation and monitoring capacity. Strong applications should address:

1. **Current Evaluation Gap**
   - What data do you currently collect?
   - What gaps exist in your evaluation approach?
   - Why is enhanced evaluation important for your program?

2. **Proposed Enhancement**
   - How will grant funds improve your evaluation capacity?
   - What specific tools, training, or systems will you implement?
   - How will this lead to better program outcomes?

3. **Stakeholder Value**
   - How will improved evaluation benefit stakeholders?
   - How will data support funding requests?
   - How will findings inform program adaptation?

4. **Sustainability**
   - How will evaluation capacity continue after grant period?
   - What systems will remain in place?
   - How will staff maintain new skills?

MHA hosts a Q&A session via Zoom before the application deadline. Prepare questions about:

3. Reporting requirements
4. Technical assistance available

## Post-Award Requirements

### Fund Utilization
- Funds must be fully expended by deadline (typically ~6 months)
- Expenditures must align with approved budget
- Keep receipts and documentation

### Reporting
- Short report due after fund utilization deadline
- Report on activities completed
- Document outcomes achieved
- Share lessons learned

### Recognition
- Acknowledge Leon Lowenstein Foundation support
- Participate in MHA communications if requested
- Share results with membership community

## FIU/NHELP Specific Preparation

### Membership Strategy

**Step 1: Confirm Eligibility**
- FIU is a state institution - verify state agency pathway
- Alternative: Apply through HWCOM as health education entity

**Step 2: Join MHA**
- Membership fee: $345
- Route through appropriate FIU department
- Register all relevant NHELP staff for access

**Step 3: Engage with Florida Coalition**
- Connect with regional network
- Attend coalition meetings
- Share NHELP best practices

### Grant Application Strategy

**Potential Project Focus Areas:**

1. **Thrive Platform Evaluation Integration**
   - Use grant to formalize evaluation methodology
   - Document AI-powered outcomes tracking
   - Create replicable evaluation framework

2. **SDOH Impact Assessment**
   - Develop tools to measure social determinants impact
   - Create patient outcome tracking system
   - Build evidence base for model

3. **Medical Student Outcomes Tracking**
   - Evaluate educational outcomes systematically
   - Document interprofessional training impact
   - Create assessment instruments

4. **Community Health Impact Measurement**
   - Formalize household health status tracking
   - Develop longitudinal outcome measures
   - Build dashboard for stakeholder reporting

### Documents to Prepare

**For Membership:**
- [ ] Organizational description (HWCOM/NHELP overview)
- [ ] Contact information for primary representative
- [ ] Payment processing through FIU

**For Grant Application:**
- [ ] NHELP program description and history
- [ ] Documentation of 10+ years of operation
- [ ] Current evaluation methodology description
- [ ] Proposed evaluation enhancement plan
- [ ] Budget for $10,000 (evaluation-focused)
- [ ] Timeline (within 6-month grant period)
- [ ] Letter of support from NHELP leadership

### Internal Approvals Needed

| Approval | Timeline |
|----------|----------|
| Department approval | Before membership |
| Dean's office | For membership and grants |
| Sponsored Programs | For grant application |
| Authorized signatory | For grant submission |
