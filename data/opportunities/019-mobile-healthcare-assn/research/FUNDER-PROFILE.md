## Mission & Vision

### Mission Statement
"To promote and serve the mobile healthcare sector through advocacy of the benefits of delivering health care using mobile platforms, education, and research in order to increase access to care for all."

### Strategic Vision
The Association is working toward a "2x by '28" goal: doubling the number of mobile health clinics in the U.S. from approximately 3,000 to 6,000 by 2028, expanding patient reach from 10 million to 20 million annually.

### Eligibility Requirements

1. Mobile Healthcare Association member in good standing
2. Currently operating a mobile program
3. Program in operation for at least one year
