## Executive Summary

The Florida Blue Foundation is one of Florida's largest health-focused philanthropic organizations with **$450 million in total assets** and annual charitable disbursements exceeding **$27 million**. Over 25 years of operation, the Foundation has awarded over **6,200 grants totaling more than $226 million**.

### Executive Compensation

The Foundation reports **$0 in officer compensation** across filing years, indicating governance through a volunteer board structure rather than salaried executives. Foundation staff are likely compensated through the parent company (Florida Blue/GuideWell).

## Strategic Implications

### For Grant Seekers

1. **Capacity Exists** - Foundation has substantial assets and sustained giving
2. **Multi-Year Preferred** - 3-4 year grants are standard model
3. **Mid-Range Sweet Spot** - Most major grants are $300K-$500K total
4. **Growth Orientation** - Foundation has increased giving over time
5. **Stable Funder** - Maintained giving despite investment volatility

### Red Flags to Watch

- None significant identified
- Investment losses have not impacted giving capacity
- Strong parent company backing provides stability
