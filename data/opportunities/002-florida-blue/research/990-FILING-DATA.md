## Mission Statement

> "To help people and communities achieve better health."

The foundation aims to improve the health and well-being of Floridians and their communities through strategic grant-making in five focus areas: improving access to healthcare, consumer health, quality and safety of patient care, quality of life, and the healthcare system.
