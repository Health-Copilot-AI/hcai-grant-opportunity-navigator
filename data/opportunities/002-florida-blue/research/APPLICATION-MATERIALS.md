## Maternal Health Grant - Specific Requirements

### Eligibility Checklist

- [ ] Florida-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization
- [ ] Documented expertise in improving maternal health outcomes
- [ ] Experience delivering maternal health services
- [ ] Capacity for 3-4 year grant implementation

### Priority Alignment Checklist

Based on Foundation's explicit priorities:

**High Priority (Explicitly Stated):**
- [ ] AI-powered solutions (chatbots, predictive analytics)
- [ ] Serving under-resourced communities
- [ ] Serving rural counties
- [ ] Focus on chronic conditions (HTN, CVD, diabetes, obesity)
- [ ] Perinatal/postpartum mental health support

**Supporting Elements:**
- [ ] Evidence-based approach
- [ ] Community partnerships
- [ ] Sustainability plan
- [ ] Measurable outcomes framework
- [ ] Cultural competency

### Recommended Narrative Elements

1. **Problem Statement**
   - Florida's maternal mortality crisis (rate higher than national average)
   - Specific community need in your service area
   - Target population demographics
   - Current gaps in services

2. **Proposed Solution**
   - AI-powered approach (if applicable)
   - Innovation elements
   - Program activities and services
   - Timeline for implementation

3. **Organizational Capacity**
   - Track record in maternal health
   - Staff qualifications
   - Infrastructure and resources
   - Prior Florida Blue Foundation grants (if any)

4. **Outcomes and Evaluation**
   - Specific, measurable outcomes
   - Data collection methods
   - Evaluation timeline
   - Learning and adaptation plan

5. **Sustainability**
   - Plan for continuation post-grant
   - Funding diversification
   - Scalability potential

## Key Success Factors

Based on analysis of successful grantees:

### Do

1. **Lead with AI** - If you have AI/technology capabilities, make them prominent
2. **Quantify Impact** - Use specific numbers for population served
3. **Show Community Roots** - Demonstrate local knowledge and relationships
4. **Provide Evidence Base** - Reference research supporting your approach
5. **Plan for Sustainability** - Show thinking beyond the grant period
6. **Connect to CDC Data** - Reference Florida's maternal health statistics
7. **Address Chronic Conditions** - Show understanding of key risk factors

### Avoid

1. **Generic Applications** - Tailor specifically to Foundation priorities
2. **Vague Outcomes** - Be specific about what you will measure
3. **Siloed Approach** - Show partnerships and collaboration
4. **Ignoring AI Preference** - At minimum, address technology use
5. **Missing Deadline** - 3:00 PM ET is firm; submit early
