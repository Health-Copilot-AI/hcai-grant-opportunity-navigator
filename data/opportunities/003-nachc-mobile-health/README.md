**Academic medical centers (including FIU HWCOM) are NOT directly eligible for NACHC grants.**

Only Section 330-funded FQHCs and FQHC Look-Alikes with NACHC membership in good standing can apply.

**Partnership Strategy Required:** FIU must partner with an eligible FQHC to access this funding. The FQHC would be the lead applicant, with NHELP providing mobile health services as a subcontractor or partner.
