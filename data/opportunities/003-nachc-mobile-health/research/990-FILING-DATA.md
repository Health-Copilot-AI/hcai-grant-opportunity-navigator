## Key Takeaways for Grant Seekers

1. **Financial Stability**: NACHC maintains strong financials with a 94% Charity Navigator score
2. **Revenue Mix**: Nearly 50/50 split between contributions and program services indicates diversified funding
3. **Not a Primary Grantmaker**: NACHC administers grants funded by external partners (Leon Lowenstein Foundation, Direct Relief) rather than making grants from its own assets
4. **Membership Organization**: Primary function is advocacy and member services, not philanthropy
5. **Well-Governed**: 100% independent board with strong oversight mechanisms

## Notes

- NACHC operates as a 501(c)(3) public charity, NOT a 501(c)(6) trade association
- Tax-deductible donations to NACHC are permitted
- The organization has been continuously tax-exempt since 1972 (50+ years)
