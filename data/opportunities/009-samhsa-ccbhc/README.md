## CCBHC Program Overview

### What is a CCBHC?
Certified Community Behavioral Health Clinics must provide:
- **Comprehensive Behavioral Health Services**
- **24/7 Crisis Services**
- **Care Coordination**
- **Integration with Primary Care**
- **Prevention, Treatment, and Recovery Services**

### 2025 CCBHC Expansion
- **State Grants:** $1M each (14 states + DC selected 2025)
- **Medicaid Demonstration:** Integration with state Medicaid programs
- **National Expansion:** CCBHC model growing rapidly

## CCBHC Certification Requirements

### Core Services
1. Crisis mental health services
2. Screening, assessment, diagnosis
3. Patient-centered treatment planning
4. Outpatient mental health services
5. Outpatient substance use disorder services
6. Primary care screening and monitoring
7. Targeted case management
8. Psychiatric rehabilitation services
9. Peer support and family services

### NHELP Gaps to Address
- 24/7 crisis services
- Substance use disorder treatment
- Psychiatric services
