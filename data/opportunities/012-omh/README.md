## Application Strategy

### COPHI Focus (Maternal Health)

**Key Elements:**
1. Community-based perinatal health model
2. Addressing SDOH barriers during pregnancy
3. Minority population focus (Hispanic, Black)
4. Community health worker integration
5. Measurable maternal/infant outcomes

### Recommended Framing

> "This demonstration project leverages FIU Thrive's AI-powered SDOH screening and NeighborhoodHELP's community-based mobile health infrastructure to address perinatal health disparities among Miami-Dade's Hispanic and Black mothers, implementing evidence-based interventions to improve maternal and infant outcomes..."
