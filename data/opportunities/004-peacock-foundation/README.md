## Application Strategy

### Recommended Framing (from background research)

**Key Points to Emphasize:**
1. **Local Miami-Dade Impact** - Community-based approach
2. **Primary Care Expansion** - Through NeighborhoodHELP
3. **Vulnerable Populations** - Uninsured, underserved residents
4. **Catalytic Investment** - Leveraged with other funding sources
5. **Clear Health/Social Outcomes** - Achievable in 1-2 year period

### Letter of Inquiry Template

> "Dear Ms. Allen,
>
> NeighborhoodHELP, Florida International University's mobile health program, requests consideration for a [amount] grant to [specific project/expansion]. Serving Miami-Dade County's underserved communities for over 15 years, we have delivered [X] health encounters and connected [Y] residents to essential services.
>
> This catalytic investment would enable us to [specific outcome], leveraging our existing infrastructure and additional funding from [other sources]. We anticipate [measurable outcomes] within [timeframe].
>
> We welcome the opportunity to discuss this further..."
