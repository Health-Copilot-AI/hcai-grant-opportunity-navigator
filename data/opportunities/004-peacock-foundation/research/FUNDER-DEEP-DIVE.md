## Executive Summary

Peacock Foundation Inc is a well-established Miami-Dade County private foundation with over 75 years of grantmaking history. With total assets exceeding $51 million and annual distributions of approximately $1.5-2 million, it represents a significant local funder for health, education, and human services organizations. The foundation maintains a strong preference for program support over capital projects and prioritizes organizations serving Miami-Dade County residents.

## Application Process

### Eligibility Requirements
1. IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) public charity (not private foundation)
2. Registered with Florida Department of Agriculture (when applicable)
3. Located in or significantly benefiting Miami-Dade County residents

### Application Steps

**Step 1: Letter of Inquiry (LOI)**
- Submit via U.S. Mail (not email)
- Include:
  - Organization mission
  - Project description with intended outcomes
  - Community served and grant period
  - Funding amount requested and intended use

**Step 2: Invitation to Apply**
- Staff reviews LOI for eligibility and merit
- If approved, receive email invitation with deadline

**Step 3: Full Proposal Submission**
- Submit complete proposal online by designated deadline

**Step 4: Site Visit**
- Foundation staff schedules on-site visit

**Step 5: Board Review**
- Board reviews proposals quarterly (4 times per year)

**Step 6: Decision Notification**
- Email notification immediately following board decision

**Step 7: Grant Agreement**
- Leadership signs electronic agreement before payment

### Key Process Notes
- **No LOI deadline** - submit any time
- **No phone/email inquiries** - U.S. Mail only for initial contact
- **Multi-year grants** available but not to be viewed as continuing support
- **Reporting required** - narrative and financial reports mandatory
- **New proposals blocked** until all prior grant requirements met

## Strategic Insights for Applicants

### What They Value
1. **Trust-based relationships** - "Peacock Foundation trusts us to know what we need"
2. **Demonstrated outcomes** - Focus on measurable impact
3. **Community responsiveness** - Increased giving during COVID-19
4. **Long-term partnerships** - Some relationships spanning decades
5. **Fiscal responsibility** - Alignment with founder's values

### Success Factors
- Strong Miami-Dade County community benefit
- Clear, measurable outcomes
- Alignment with health, education, or human services
- Program support (not capital requests)
- Organizational capacity and track record
- Reasonable ask within typical range ($25,000-$60,000)

### Potential Challenges
- Mail-only initial contact (no quick email inquiry)
- Competitive process (~50 awards from larger applicant pool)
- No stated deadlines creates uncertainty
- Must wait for invitation before full proposal
- Prior grantees must complete all requirements before reapplying

### Tips from Analysis
1. Research their featured grantees for alignment examples
2. Emphasize Miami-Dade County impact specifically
3. Frame requests around "well-being of children and families"
4. Demonstrate fiscal responsibility and sustainability
5. Prepare for site visit as part of standard process
6. Keep initial LOI concise and compelling
