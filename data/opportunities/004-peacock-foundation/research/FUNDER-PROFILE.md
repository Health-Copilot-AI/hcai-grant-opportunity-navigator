## Mission and Vision

### Mission Statement

> "To enhance and promote the good health and well-being of children and families in Miami-Dade County through grants to nonprofit organizations."

### Founding Values

The foundation's grantmaking reflects Henry B. Peacock Jr.'s beliefs in:
1. Strong values and moral character
2. Achieving self-sufficiency and helping others
3. Fiscal responsibility as philanthropic stewards

### Historical Context

- Over 75 years of continuous grantmaking
- Supported 500+ nonprofit organizations
- Distributed $50+ million in total grants
- Deliberately maintains low profile, preferring grantees receive attention

## Application Process

### Eligibility Requirements

1. IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) public charity (not private foundation)
2. Registered with Florida Department of Agriculture (when applicable)
3. Located in or significantly benefiting Miami-Dade County residents

### Application Steps

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Letter of Inquiry (LOI)** | Submit via U.S. Mail (not email) |
| **2. Invitation to Apply** | Staff reviews LOI; if approved, receive email invitation |
| **3. Full Proposal** | Submit complete proposal online by deadline |
| **4. Site Visit** | Foundation staff conducts on-site visit |
| **5. Board Review** | Board reviews proposals quarterly |
| **6. Decision Notification** | Email notification following board decision |
| **7. Grant Agreement** | Electronic agreement signed before payment |

### Key Process Notes

- **No LOI deadline** - submit any time
- **No phone/email inquiries** - U.S. Mail only for initial contact
- **Quarterly review** - Board meets 4 times per year
- **Site visit required** - Standard part of evaluation
