## About CDC SDOH Accelerator

- **Purpose:** Planning grants to develop implementation-ready SDOH plans
- **Focus:** Policy, system, environmental, and programmatic changes
- **Goal:** Improved chronic disease outcomes among underserved populations
- **Award Size:** ~$125,000 for 12-month planning grants
- **Historical Awards:** 71 total recipients (2021-2024)

## Application Strategy

### SDOH Domain Focus

**Primary Domain: Community-Clinical Linkages**
- FIU Thrive screening in community settings
- Referral to clinical services
- Closed-loop follow-up
- CHW navigation support

**Secondary Domain: Food and Nutrition Security**
- Food insecurity screening
- Connection to food assistance programs
- Partnership with food banks/pantries

### Recommended Framing

> "This Accelerator Plan will leverage FIU Thrive's AI-powered SDOH screening platform to develop implementation-ready strategies for strengthening community-clinical linkages and addressing food insecurity in Miami-Dade County's underserved communities, creating a replicable model for chronic disease prevention through upstream social determinant interventions..."
