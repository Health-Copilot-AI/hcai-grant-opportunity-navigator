## Opportunity Summary

The Dell Foundation makes substantial investments in **evidence-based, scalable solutions** for families and children. Health Innovation is an explicit priority area with demonstrated interest in AI/technology solutions.

### Funding Priorities
1. **Health Innovation** - Technology-enabled health solutions
2. Education - K-12 and higher education access
3. Economic Stability - Family financial security

### Key Requirements
- Evidence-based approach
- Scalable model
- Measurable outcomes
- Long-term sustainability
