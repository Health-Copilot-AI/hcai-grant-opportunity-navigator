## Application Process Overview

### Two-Stage Process

| Stage | Purpose | System |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Letter of Intent (LOI)** | Initial screening | PCORI Online |
| **Full Application** | Complete proposal (invitation only) | PCORI Online |

### Process Flow

```
LOI Submission (Required)
        |
        v
Administrative Review
        |
        v
Programmatic Review (by PCORI Staff)
        |
        v
LOI Status Notification (~30 days)
        |
        v [If Invited]
Full Application Submission
        |
        v
Merit Review (Scientists + Patients + Stakeholders)
        |
        v
Post-Panel PCORI Staff Review
        |
        v
Selection Committee Recommendation
        |
        v
Executive Director Approval
        |
        v
Award Notification
```

## Letter of Intent Requirements

### LOI Components (All Research Awards)

| Component | Description | Purpose |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Research Question** | Clear, patient-centered comparative question | Define scope |
| **Background** | Current evidence gaps and need | Justify importance |
| **Study Population** | Target patients, settings, geographic scope | Define reach |
| **Comparators** | Two or more interventions being compared | Establish design |
| **Outcomes** | Patient-centered outcome measures | Demonstrate relevance |
| **Study Design** | Methodological approach (RCT, observational, etc.) | Show rigor |
| **Sample Size/Power** | Statistical justification | Ensure feasibility |
| **Analytical Plan** | Statistical methods and HTE analysis | Demonstrate methodology |
| **Engagement Strategy** | Patient/stakeholder involvement plan | Address requirements |
| **PI/Team Qualifications** | Experience and expertise | Show capability |
| **Duration and Costs** | Project scope and budget estimate | Enable planning |
| **References** | Supporting literature | Document evidence base |

### LOI Templates by Program

| Program | Template Name | Download Location |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| Broad Pragmatic Studies | BPS LOI Template | PFA page |
| PLACER | PLACER LOI Template | PFA page |
| Methods Research | Methods LOI Template | PFA page |
| Retrospective Observational | Observational LOI Template | PFA page |
| Engagement Awards | Engagement LOI Template | PFA page |

### LOI Formatting Requirements

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| **Page Limit** | Varies by PFA (check specific announcement) |
| **Font** | As specified in template |
| **Margins** | As specified in template |
| **References** | Excluded from page limit |
| **Format** | PDF upload to PCORI Online |

### LOI Review Criteria

**Administrative Review:**
- Compliance with PFA guidelines
- Adherence to formatting requirements
- Page limit compliance
- Completeness of submission

**Programmatic Review:**
- Alignment with PFA priorities
- Responsiveness to what PCORI is seeking
- Two or more PCORI staff reviewers
- All LOI template items reviewed

## Budget Requirements

### Budget Template Components

#### Personnel Section

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| **Salary Cap** | $200,000 annual institutional base salary |
| **Effort Calculation** | Percentage of salary cap, not actual salary |
| **Fringe Benefits** | No cap; use institutional rate |
| **Student Salaries** | Allowed |
| **Student Stipends** | Not allowed |

#### Other Direct Costs

| Category | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **Equipment** | Items >$5,000 allowed with justification |
| **Travel** | Must follow Uniform Guidance (2 CFR 200.474) |
| **Supplies** | Standard justification required |
| **Consultant Costs** | Reasonable rates required |
| **Patient Care Costs** | >15% of budget requires strong justification |
| **DSMB Expenses** | Limited to 2% of direct costs |
| **IRB Costs** | Allowable as direct cost |

#### Subcontract Section

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| **First $25,000** | Included in prime's indirect cost base |
| **Above $25,000** | Excluded from indirect cost base |
| **Fixed-Fee/Capitated** | Allowed with clear delineation |
| **Detailed Justification** | Required for all subcontracts |

#### Indirect Costs

| Applicant Type | Maximum Rate |
|----------------|--------------|
| **Domestic with Federal Rate** | 40% or negotiated rate (whichever is lower) |
| **Domestic without Federal Rate** | 10% |
| **Foreign Applicants** | 10% |

#### Exclusions from Indirect Cost Base

- Equipment
- Patient care costs
- Subcontractor costs above $25,000

### Budget by Program

| Program | Budget Maximum | Duration |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| **BPS Category 1** | $5M direct | Up to 5 years |
| **BPS Category 2** | $12M direct | Up to 5 years |
| **BPS Category 3** | $12M direct | Up to 5 years |
| **PLACER** | $22M direct total | ~6.5 years |
| **Methods** | $750K direct | Up to 3 years |
| **Retrospective** | $2M direct | Up to 18 months |
| **Engagement** | $300K total | Up to 2 years |

## Methodology Standards Checklist

### Purpose

Ensure research projects contain PCORI Methodology Standards as defined by the Methodology Committee and adopted by the Board of Governors.

### Format

- **File Type:** Excel (.xlsx)
- **Submission:** Upload as PDF with Research Plan

### Categories to Address

#### Cross-Cutting Standards (32 Standards)

| Code Range | Category | Standards |
|------------|----------|-----------|
| RQ-1 to RQ-6 | Formulating Research Questions | 5 |
| PC-1 to PC-4 | Patient Centeredness | 4 |
| IR-1 to IR-7 | Data Integrity and Rigorous Analyses | 7 |
| MD-1 to MD-4 | Preventing and Handling Missing Data | 4 |
| HT-1 to HT-3 | Heterogeneity of Treatment Effects | 3 |
| UC-1 to UC-2 | Usual Care as Comparator | 2 |

#### Design-Specific Standards (35 Standards)

| Code Range | Category | Standards |
|------------|----------|-----------|
| DR-1 to DR-4 | Data Registries | 4 |
| DN-1 to DN-2 | Data Networks | 2 |
| CI-1 to CI-6 | Causal Inference Methods | 6 |
| AT-1 to AT-4 | Adaptive and Bayesian Trial Designs | 4 |
| MT-1 to MT-3 | Medical Tests | 3 |
| SR-1 | Systematic Reviews | 1 |
| RC-1 to RC-5 | Cluster-Based Research | 5 |
| SCI-1 to SCI-5 | Complex Interventions | 5 |
| QM-1 to QM-4 | Qualitative Methods | 4 |
| MM-1 to MM-3 | Mixed Methods Research | 3 |
| IPD-1 to IPD-4 | Individual Participant-Level Data Meta-Analysis | 4 |

### Completion Instructions

1. Download checklist from PCORI resources
2. Review all applicable standards for your study design
3. Indicate how each relevant standard is addressed
4. Document any deviations with justification
5. Save as PDF for upload

## Patient Engagement Documentation

### Foundational Expectations Checklist

Applications must address all six expectations:

| Expectation | Documentation Required |
|-------------|----------------------|
| **1. Representative Involvement** | Description of partner diversity and representation |
| **2. Early & Ongoing Engagement** | Timeline of partner involvement throughout study |
| **3. Dedicated Funds & Compensation** | Budget line items for engagement and partner payment |
| **4. Build Capacity as Team** | Training and support plans |
| **5. Meaningful Decision-Making** | Governance structures for partner input |
| **6. Ongoing Assessment** | Evaluation plan for engagement activities |

### Patient/Stakeholder Partner Template Contents

| Section | Content Required |
|---------|------------------|
| **Partner Identification** | Name, role, organization |
| **Lived Experience** | Relevant patient/community connection |
| **Expertise** | Skills and knowledge contributed |
| **Role in Project** | Specific responsibilities |
| **Engagement Level** | Input, consultation, collaboration, or shared leadership |
| **Time Commitment** | Hours per month/year |
| **Compensation** | How and how much partner is paid |

### Engagement Budget Requirements

| Item | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Partner Compensation** | Must be budgeted explicitly |
| **Meeting Costs** | Travel, accommodations, childcare |
| **Training Materials** | Educational resources for partners |
| **Communication Tools** | Technology for remote participation |
| **Accessibility Supports** | Translation, ASL, accommodations |

### Engagement Assessment Plan

Required within months 12-18 of study:
- Document engagement activities completed
- Assess effectiveness of engagement approaches
- Gather partner feedback
- Identify improvements needed
- Plan adjustments based on assessment

## Submission System: PCORI Online

### System Access

- **URL:** https://pcori.my.site.com
- **Account Required:** Yes, create account before submission
- **Recommended:** Set up account well before deadline

### Submission Process

1. **Create Account** - Register in PCORI Online
2. **Start Application** - Select appropriate PFA
3. **Complete Forms** - Fill out online components
4. **Upload Documents** - Attach required templates
5. **Review** - Check completeness
6. **Submit** - Before deadline (5 pm ET)

### System Requirements

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Browser** | Current version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge |
| **File Formats** | PDF (most documents), Excel (budget, checklist) |
| **File Size** | Check PFA for limits |
| **Naming** | Follow any naming conventions specified |

## Review Process and Criteria

### Merit Review Scoring

| Score Range | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| **1-3** | Minor weaknesses (fundable range) |
| **4-6** | Moderate weaknesses |
| **7-9** | Major weaknesses (not fundable) |

### Review Panel Composition

| Reviewer Type | Role |
|---------------|------|
| **Scientific Reviewers** | Evaluate methodology and rigor |
| **Patient Reviewers** | Assess patient-centeredness |
| **Stakeholder Reviewers** | Evaluate relevance and impact |

All reviewer scores are weighted equally.

### Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Key Questions |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Scientific Rigor** | Is the methodology sound? Are standards met? |
| **Patient-Centeredness** | Are outcomes important to patients? |
| **Engagement Quality** | Are partnerships meaningful and sustained? |
| **Feasibility** | Can the team execute the proposed research? |
| **Potential Impact** | Will findings inform decision-making? |
| **Innovation** | Does the study advance the field? |
