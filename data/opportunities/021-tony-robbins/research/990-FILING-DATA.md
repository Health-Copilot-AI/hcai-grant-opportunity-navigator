## Mission Statement (from 990)

> The Tony Robbins Foundation is a nonprofit organization created to empower individuals and organizations to make a significant difference in the quality of life of people often forgotten. The Foundation works globally to uplift and support vulnerable populations such as youth, seniors, the homeless, the hungry, and individuals within correctional facilities.
