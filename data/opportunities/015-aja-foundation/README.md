## Opportunity Summary

The AJA Foundation focuses on addressing **structural barriers** to fundamental resources - food, housing, healthcare, and education. The foundation prioritizes root cause solutions rather than direct service delivery.

### Funding Priorities
- Access to fundamental resources
- Structural barrier removal
- Systems change approaches
- Community-driven solutions

### Key Requirements
- 501(c)(3) status
- LOI required before full application
- Maximum 1 request per organization per year
- Focus on structural/systemic change
